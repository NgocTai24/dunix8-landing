# Content Guide

## San pham

Moi san pham can co `title`, `description`, `category`, `categorySlug`, `volume`, `fragrance`, `price`, `image`, `featured` va `benefits`.

## Tin tuc

Moi bai viet can co `title`, `description`, `date`, `category` va `image`.

Noi dung nen viet ngan gon, de doc, uu tien loi ich thuc te cho gia dinh va dai ly.
